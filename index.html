About the test:

An Embarrassment test is a self-graded survey that assesses the participants' history of embarrassing behaviors (ex: engaging in sexual activity with white cis men). The test scores the most embarrassing as 100/100 and the least embarrassing a 0/100. Simply put, the Embarrassment Test tells you how many checkboxes you clicked. We pray that you do not click that many. The Embarrassment test uses questions taylored to college students, but all are welcome to take the test.


Instructions:

Simply click on the checkboxes that apply to you. At the end submit your response and get a score. As you go through the trainwreck that is life, take the test again and compare your score. None of the topics in this test are supported or recommended. This is merely a way to illustrate how embarrassed you should be.
